How to add percentages

How to Add Percentages: Methods and Examples

Adding percentages is a fundamental mathematical operation that can be approached in two distinct methods.

Method 1: Adding Percentage Values Directly

  1. Convert percentages to decimal form: If a percentage is given, convert it to its decimal form by dividing it by 100. For example, 25% becomes 0.25.

  2. Add the decimal values: Simply add the decimal values obtained from the percentages. For instance, \(25\% + 15\% = 0.25 + 0.15 = 0.40\) (40%).

Method 2: Calculating Percentages and Then Adding

  1. Find the percentages of the numbers: Multiply each number by its corresponding percentage in decimal form.

  2. Add the results: Sum up the values obtained from step 1. For example, \(150 \times 0.20 + 200 \times 0.15 = 30 + 30 = 60\).

Choose the method that best fits your scenario. If you already have percentages in decimal form, method 1 might be simpler. If you have the original numbers and percentages, method 2 is more suitable.
